About me

My journey in web design and front-end development began in December 2015 from working on my own HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Before that I worked as an account manager in a staffing agency and have gained extensive experience in customer service.

Driven by curiosity and eagerness to learn something new, I dove into web development and realized the beauty of coding and the power of web design principles. I am passionate about typography and creating design with web accessibility in mind.

If you are interested in working with me or have any questions — email me or find in socials.
